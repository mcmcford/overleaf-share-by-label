The overleaf-teams release has been installed.

This chart runs the sync process as a CronJob.

Useful commands:
  kubectl get cronjob {{ include "overleaf-teams.fullname" . }}
  kubectl get jobs --selector app.kubernetes.io/instance={{ .Release.Name }}
  kubectl logs job/<job-name>

If you use an existing secret, it must expose these keys:
  authentik-token
  overleaf-mongo-uri
  overleaf-mongo-username
  overleaf-mongo-password
